About Book Room

Book Room is a website where you can search through all the books we have by a key word in the title, authors name or genre. You can also edit the details of each book, delete a book, or mark a book as urgent if it is due back at the library. Or go to the add a book page to add a book.

Enjoy all the features of the website. Happy reading!
